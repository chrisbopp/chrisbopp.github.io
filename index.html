About Chris

PhD Student, Technologist, Data Geek, Idealist

I love technology but hate injustice. I study ways that technology can help solve the world's most pressing problems at the University of Colorado's ATLAS Institute with my advisor Dr. Amy Voida. I conduct research in human-computer interaction and computer supported cooperative work, with a focus on philanthropic informatics. I was a Data Science for Social Good Fellow at the University of Chicago, and formerly managed technology at the AIDS Foundation of Chicago.

Across all my work, I try to find ways to better understand people in order to help facilitate a more fair and just world.

I live in the beautiful state of Colorado with my amazing wife and incredibly drooly cat. I love finding efficient ways to address inefficiencies. I love spending the day with a cup of coffee learning about anything and everything. Sometimes I think I should go outside more.
